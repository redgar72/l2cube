Wide Turns Breakdown

Wide turns rotate two layers together (the outer layer and the middle layer) as if they were one piece.

r (Right Wide)

Rotates the right face and middle layer together 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Right face + middle layer
  • Equivalent to: R + M' (but done as one move)
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the right face
  • Prime: r' rotates counterclockwise

u (Up Wide)

Rotates the top face and middle layer together 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Top face + middle layer
  • Equivalent to: U + E' (but done as one move)
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking down at the top face
  • Prime: u' rotates counterclockwise

f (Front Wide)

Rotates the front face and middle layer together 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Front face + middle layer
  • Equivalent to: F + S (but done as one move)
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the front face
  • Prime: f' rotates counterclockwise

l (Left Wide)

Rotates the left face and middle layer together 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Left face + middle layer
  • Equivalent to: L + M (but done as one move)
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the left face
  • Prime: l' rotates counterclockwise

d (Down Wide)

Rotates the bottom face and middle layer together 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Bottom face + middle layer
  • Equivalent to: D + E (but done as one move)
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking up at the bottom face
  • Prime: d' rotates counterclockwise

b (Back Wide)

Rotates the back face and middle layer together 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Back face + middle layer
  • Equivalent to: B + S' (but done as one move)
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the back face
  • Prime: b' rotates counterclockwise
