Face Turns Breakdown

Face turns rotate the outer layer of each face 90 degrees clockwise (or counterclockwise for prime moves).

R (Right Face)

Rotates the right face 90° clockwise. The right face turns toward you.

  • Affects: Right face, top edge, front edge, bottom edge, back edge
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the right face
  • Prime: R' rotates counterclockwise

U (Up Face)

Rotates the top face 90° clockwise. The top face turns to the right.

  • Affects: Top face, top edges of all four sides
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking down at the top face
  • Prime: U' rotates counterclockwise

F (Front Face)

Rotates the front face 90° clockwise. The front face turns to the right.

  • Affects: Front face, top edge, right edge, bottom edge, left edge
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the front face
  • Prime: F' rotates counterclockwise

L (Left Face)

Rotates the left face 90° clockwise. The left face turns away from you.

  • Affects: Left face, top edge, back edge, bottom edge, front edge
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the left face
  • Prime: L' rotates counterclockwise

D (Down Face)

Rotates the bottom face 90° clockwise. The bottom face turns to the right.

  • Affects: Bottom face, bottom edges of all four sides
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking up at the bottom face
  • Prime: D' rotates counterclockwise

B (Back Face)

Rotates the back face 90° clockwise. The back face turns to the right.

  • Affects: Back face, top edge, left edge, bottom edge, right edge
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the back face
  • Prime: B' rotates counterclockwise
