Slice Turns Breakdown

Slice turns rotate only the middle layer of the cube, leaving the outer faces unchanged.

M (Middle Slice)

Rotates the middle layer between the left and right faces 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Only the middle layer
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the right face
  • Equivalent to: L' R (but done as one move)
  • Prime: M' rotates counterclockwise
  • Double: M2 rotates 180°

E (Equatorial Slice)

Rotates the middle layer between the top and bottom faces 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Only the middle layer
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking down at the top face
  • Equivalent to: U D' (but done as one move)
  • Prime: E' rotates counterclockwise
  • Double: E2 rotates 180°

S (Standing Slice)

Rotates the middle layer between the front and back faces 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Only the middle layer
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking at the front face
  • Equivalent to: F B' (but done as one move)
  • Prime: S' rotates counterclockwise
  • Double: S2 rotates 180°
