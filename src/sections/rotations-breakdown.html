Rotations Breakdown

Rotations turn the entire cube around one of its axes, changing your perspective without affecting the solved state.

x (X-axis Rotation)

Rotates the entire cube around the X-axis (left-right axis) 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Entire cube
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking from left to right
  • Result: Top face becomes front face
  • Prime: x' rotates counterclockwise
  • Double: x2 rotates 180°

y (Y-axis Rotation)

Rotates the entire cube around the Y-axis (up-down axis) 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Entire cube
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking from bottom to top
  • Result: Front face becomes left face
  • Prime: y' rotates counterclockwise
  • Double: y2 rotates 180°

z (Z-axis Rotation)

Rotates the entire cube around the Z-axis (front-back axis) 90° clockwise.

  • Affects: Entire cube
  • Direction: Clockwise when looking from back to front
  • Result: Top face becomes right face
  • Prime: z' rotates counterclockwise
  • Double: z2 rotates 180°
